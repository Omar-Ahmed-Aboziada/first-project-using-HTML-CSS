About us

Coffe Hub is a Sydney based specialty coffee roastery & lifestyle store.



Founded by Jodie Foster and Dean Wooding in 2015, Skittle Lane blends their love of quality coffee, iconic design and great hospitality.

Coffe Hub is named after the alleyway that runs beside our first flagship venue in Sydney's CBD.
But five years on, the brand now also encompasses an in-house roastery in Bondi Beach, a curated collection of brew gear and homewares, and a community of select cafes and businesses
around Australia that serve our beans.

The Coffe Hub team are highly passionate about sourcing and sharing the best, most ethically produced coffee from around the world. We take huge pride and care in every step of the journey - all the way from selecting green coffee, right through to our recyclable and biodegradable packaging.
